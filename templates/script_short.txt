SERIES: YouTube Shorts / Instagram Reels
TARGET DURATION: 30-60 seconds (75-150 words at 150 wpm)
FORMAT: Vertical 9:16 (1080x1920)

STRUCTURE:
- HOOK (0-3 sec): Bold text overlay + opening statement
  Must grab attention INSTANTLY. No intro, no greeting.
  Examples:
    "This AI trade made me $X"
    "Stop using [tool] — here's why"
    "I built this in 60 seconds with AI"
- VALUE (5-45 sec): ONE key insight, result, or tip
  Fast-paced, no filler. Every second must deliver value.
  Use jump cuts. Keep energy high.
- CTA (3-5 sec): "Follow for more" or "Full video on YouTube"
  Point to long-form for deeper content.

TEXT OVERLAY RULES:
- Always add text overlays (many watch without sound)
- Font: Bold, white with dark outline
- Position: Center or lower third
- Max 6 words per overlay
- Change overlay every 3-5 seconds

TYPES OF SHORTS:
1. RESULTS REVEAL: "My AI made/lost $X this week" (extract from build log)
2. QUICK TIP: "One Claude Code trick you need to know"
3. HOT TAKE: "Why [tool] is overrated"
4. TIMELAPSE BUILD: "Building [X] in 60 seconds"
5. BEFORE/AFTER: "Before AI vs After AI"
6. MISTAKE: "This mistake cost my AI $X"

INSTAGRAM-SPECIFIC:
- Add 20-30 relevant hashtags in caption
- First line of caption is the hook (shows in feed)
- Tag relevant accounts (@anthropic, etc.)
- Use trending audio when available (manual step)
